Chicken Recipes

Description

Chicken is a popular meat in Nigeria. It is often used in a variety of dishes, including jollof rice, fried rice, and pepper soup. Chicken is a good source of protein and is often served with a side of salad or fried plantains.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Add the curry powder, thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Stir well and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Add the chicken stock and water.
  8. Cover the pot and cook for 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  9. Remove the chicken from the pot and serve hot.
