Nigerian Salad recipes

Description

Nigerian salad comprises of different types of fresh vegetables, boiled eggs, corned beef or sardines and Heinz salad cream or mayonnaise is usually used as dressing for Nigerian salad. Nigerian salad comprises of different types of fresh vegetables, boiled eggs, corned beef or sardines and Heinz salad cream or mayonnaise is usually used as dressing for Nigerian salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 1 can of corned beef or sardines
  • Heinz salad cream or mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce.
  2. Peel and dice the cucumber.
  3. Chop the tomatoes and onion.
  4. Peel and slice the boiled eggs.
  5. Open the can of corned beef or sardines.
  6. Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl.
  7. Add the salad cream or mayonnaise and toss to combine.
  8. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
