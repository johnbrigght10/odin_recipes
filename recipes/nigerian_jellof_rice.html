Nigerian Jellof Rice Recipes

Description

Jollof rice is a popular dish in Nigeria. It is made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a variety of spices. It is often served with fried plantains and a side of salad. Jellof rice made in nigeria by the Nigerian style is popullarly known as "Nigerian jellof rice" and is romoured to be the best jellof rice in the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and set aside.
  2. Blend the onion, bell pepper, and scotch bonnet pepper until smooth.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  4. Add the blended pepper mixture and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the tomato paste, chicken stock, water, curry powder, thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Stir well and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Add the rice and stir well to combine.
  8. Cover the pot and cook for 20 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
  9. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
